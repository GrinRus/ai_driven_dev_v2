# Document Contract: `idea-brief.md`

## Purpose

Capture the problem statement, desired outcome, constraints, and open questions.

## Required sections

- `Problem statement`
- `Desired outcome`
- `Constraints`
- `Open questions`

## Validation cues

- the file exists in the expected stage directory,
- the required headings are present,
- the content is non-placeholder and stage-relevant,
- problem framing and desired outcomes are grounded in provided inputs, with source-level root
  causes left to research unless already evidenced by context,
- upstream references are present when the stage requires them.

## Notes

This is a Markdown contract, not a runtime-output JSON schema.
